My name is Antonella Tamburrano, I was born in Italy and I am based in Edinburgh.

It is hard to define what I do as my interests are always shifting and my mind is easily attracted by so many interesting things, some of which I do not know and which I’d like to learn. To put it simply, I am mostly a digital designer, and I like creating all forms of visuals and interactions.

I recently finished a BSc with Honours in Digital Media and Design at Edinburgh Napier University, Edinburgh.

I’m open to opportunities and collaborations, feel free to drop me a message!
