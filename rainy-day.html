Rainy Day

MUSIC VIDEO PRODUCTION

Rainy Day is a video production piece of work that is supposed to represent one's own visual interpretation of the lyrics of a song of choice, which is incorporated in the narrative structure of the video itself. Following the pre-production stage and the creation of a storybpard (which can be seen below), the production team sat together to plan for the shootings, the permissions and the location scouting. The post-production involved quite a considerable amount of extra-work to execute in order to meet the deadlines of the production, as the rendering of some 3D assets took more time than previously predicted.
